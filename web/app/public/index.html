◆ Local by mechanism· ↗ Private by default· ≡ Everything on the record

The warmth of a personal assistant — without the cloud.

An assistant that sees, hears, and helps, that lives on your hardware instead of someone else's data center. Private because of how it's built, not because of a promise.

◆

Local by mechanism

Models run on your machine. The daemons that read your context have no network access at all — privacy enforced by the system, not by policy.

↗

Private by default

Nothing offloads to a provider unless you switch it on, per scope. When something would leave your hardware, it's marked in a distinct amber — you always know.

≡

Yours, on the record

Every model call, every piece of context read, every action taken is written to an append-only Ledger you can read in plain English. No black box.

Alpha, in the open

What works today.

We build in the open and we're honest about the state. Real, on real hardware, now:

Working now

  • Bootable, self-updating immutable OS (A/B slots, auto-rollback)
  • Local inference + guided generation as a system service
  • Append-only audit Ledger, enforced by the system
  • On-device context search with per-app permissions
  • Bring-your-own cloud providers — signed in, off by default
  • A chat assistant: local and cloud models, streaming, ledgered

On the way

  • Native AI panel in Settings for models & providers
  • Always-on ambient assistant
  • Apps you can generate and install locally
  • Voice + wake-word loop, fully on device
